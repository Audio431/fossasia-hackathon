How it works

Three layers of protection

All detection runs locally in your browser. Nothing leaves the device.

Step 01
⌨️

Watch every keystroke

A lightweight content script attaches to every text input on every website, running real-time analysis as the child types — debounced to avoid lag.

Step 02
🔍

Detect PII & grooming

Two detection engines run in parallel: a 30+ regex PII detector for personal data, and a 7-category heuristic grooming detector for stranger danger signals.

Step 03
🚨

Alert the child instantly

A full-screen overlay interrupts the action before submission with a kid-friendly explanation. Parents see a persistent alert in the dashboard.

Step 04
🔒

100% local, zero telemetry

No external API calls. No data collection. Alert history stays in chrome.storage.local on the user's machine only.

PII Detection Engine

Catches what kids
forget to protect

30+ regex patterns across 7 categories, with context-aware severity scoring.

📅

Birth Date & Age

Date formats, written ages, grade levels, birthday expressions.

turning 14 03/15/2010 7th grade
📍

Location

City + state combos, GPS coordinates, "I live in…" patterns.

Springfield, IL live in Austin
📞

Contact Info

Phone numbers in all formats, email addresses.

(555) 867-5309 user@email.com
🏠

Home Address

Street number + road type combos, "at 123 Main St".

123 Elm Street at 45 Oak Ave
🏫

School & Team

"I go to Lincoln Middle", sports team names with context clues.

I go to… I attend…
💳

Financial Data

Credit card numbers, SSNs, bank account numbers.

4111 1111… SSN: 123-45
🪪

Government ID

Passport numbers, US license plate patterns.

passport A12345678 ABC-1234
⚡

Smart Combo Escalation

Multiple PII types together trigger a higher risk score — e.g. financial + address = critical.

+15 multi-type cap 100

Stranger Guard

Detects grooming patterns
before it's too late

7 weighted rule categories accumulate context across multiple messages in a conversation.

Secrecy
25 pts

"Don't tell your parents about us. Keep it secret."

Meeting
30 pts

"Can we meet up in person? I'll pick you up."

Age-inappropriate
35 pts

Sexual language or content directed at a minor.

Personal info harvest
20 pts

"What school do you go to? What's your address?"

Gift / Bribe
20 pts

"I'll buy you a gift card if you send me a photo."

Isolation
20 pts

"Your friends don't understand you like I do."

Pressure / Urgency
15 pts

"Don't wait. You need to decide right now."

Score ≥ 35 → Warning overlay shown  ·  Score ≥ 60 → Danger overlay shown  ·  3+ categories → +15 bonus  ·  4+ categories → +20 bonus

Architecture

Entirely in-browser

No backend, no telemetry. The full detection pipeline runs as Chrome MV3 extension code.

Browser Tab (any website) form-monitor.ts — watches text inputs, runs PII detection stranger-monitor.ts — watches DM/chat inputs, runs grooming detection image-monitor.ts — intercepts file uploads, reads EXIF GPS ↓ chrome.runtime.sendMessage ↓ Background Service Worker (background.ts) • Routes messages → FORM_SUBMISSION, STRANGER_RISK_DETECTED, IMAGE_UPLOAD • Checks settings → enabled flag, sensitivity threshold, quiet hours • Stores alerts → chrome.storage.local (persisted, cap 50) • Updates badge → chrome.action.setBadgeText ↓ GET_ALERTS on popup open ↓ Popup (popup.tsx) Parent tab → alert cards with PII/Stranger badges Kid tab → privacy score, safety tips Settings tab → enabled toggle, sensitivity, quiet hours
